Environments (O/S)

Whilst a majority of my time has been spent within the MS WIndows environment, I really enjoyed the days of the MS DOS command prompt. I have played quite a bit with Linux in the past, I even had a stint with Unix and an old Xenix server that ran a number of Wyse terminals. I helped with routine maintenance on this system.

I owned a Macbook for a few years which I loved and got used to the OS-X environment. Having the ability to open up a terminal and get to a 'Bash' style environment was appealing to me too. In my youth, we had some legacy hardware around and I had the opportunity to play with some TRS-80's which ran TRS-DOS in 5 1/4" Floppy's which was fun.
